Activities

Seoul

Lotte World

The world largest indoor amusement park. It has both an indoor theme park and outdoor amusement park.

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Built in 1395, it is located in northern Seoul, South Korea. It is the he largest of the Five Grand Palaces built by the Joseon dynasty. Gyeongbokgung served as the home of Kings of the Joseon dynasty, the Kings' households, as well as the government of Joseon. It was a former royal palace

K-Beauty Shopping

If you are into makeup and skincare, Myeongdong would be a good place to go. Korea is known for their amazing skincare and makeup products.

Banpo Bridge

This bridge is not like any other bridge. So what's so special about this bridge? The answer is the Moonlight Rainbow Fountain, the world longest bridge fountain. The water for the fountain comes directly from the river itself and is continuously recycled.

Jeju Waterworld

This waterpark is for people of different ages. There's a spa and sauna for you to relax in. The waterpark has slides and wave/ regular pools.

Manjanggul

Located in Gimnyeong-ri, Gujwaeup, Jeju City. it is the 12th-longest lava tube in the world and the second longest on Jeju island. It is regarded as having significant scientific and heritage value, owing to its excellent condition of preservation despite its age of formation.
